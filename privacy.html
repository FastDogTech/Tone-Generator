Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2024

FastDog Tech is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by our Tone Generator mobile application.

Data Collection

Tone Generator is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All audio generation and operations are performed locally on your device.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No audio data is stored or transmitted
  • No location data is collected
  • No usage analytics are tracked

App Functionality

Our app provides audio tone generation tools for testing, tuning, and audio experiments. All sound generation is performed locally on your device, and no data is saved or transmitted to external servers.

Data Security

As no data is stored or transmitted, there is no risk of data breach or unauthorized access to sensitive information.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs that would collect or process your information.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any information from any person, including children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and in the app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store or via email at info@fastdog.tech
